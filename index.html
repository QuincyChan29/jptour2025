Departure @ Hong Kong International Airport

Let the learning journey begin!

Visiting Kansai University

Our group had the opportunity to visit the prestigious Kansai University Senriyama Campus.

Campus tour @ Kansai University

We received a warm welcome and a comprehensive, student-led tour that showcased key campus facilities and shared valuable insights into the school's culture and its major sporting events.

Plus Ultra Mechanism Lab @ The University of Osaka

We visited the Plus Ultra Mechanism Lab, where we learned that students get hands-on robotics experience by designing and building their own projects with tools like 3D printers.

Company Visit @ Kanadevia A.I/TEC

The visit to Kanadevia in Japan was an insightful experience that offered a deep understanding of the company's history, its cutting-edge AI technology, and the broader landscape of Japanese corporate culture and innovation.

Visting Kyoto University

We visited Kyoto University Yoshida Campus, where we walked through its historic grounds and saw the iconic Clock Tower Centennial Hall, a symbol of the institution's rich academic legacy.

Computer Vision Lab @ Kyoto University

During our visit to the computer vision lab, we experienced several impressive demos that gave us a firsthand look at their cutting-edge projects.

HRI Lab @ Kyoto University

At the human-robot interaction lab, we saw several fascinating robots and learned about their real-world applications through various case studies of their interactions with people.

Discovering the Osaka Expo

We went to the Osaka Expo and wandered through a diverse array of fascinating pavilions.

Exploring Osaka Castle Park

We visited a local kimono shop first, then made our way to the Osaka Castle Park.

Visiting Wakayama Castle and Rinku Park

We had a wonderful day exploring the culture and natural scenery in Wakayama City and Rinku Town.

Experiencing the legendary Suzuka Circuit

The highlight of the day was getting the chance to drive a round trip on the actual Formula 1 circuit.

Wandering in Osaka & Katsuoji Temple

We went to Katsuoji Temple, a place revered for granting "winning luck," where we saw thousands of Daruma dolls and enjoyed activities like creating our own postcards and feeding koi carp.

One-day Tour of Osaka Attractions

We explored Osaka's Sky Garden, Osaka Castle, and Shitennoji Temple, enjoyed the views from Sakishima Observatory, and relaxed at Hinata no Yu hot springs at night. On this day, we experienced both Japan's traditional culture and modern life.

Home

The Japan Study Tour 2025 was organized by the City University Computer Science Department from 2 - 6 June 2025, leading students to visit local universities and companies and engage in cultural activities. The trip was supervised by Dr. Howard Leung and Dr. Kenneth Lee

The journey began with a flight from Hong Kong to Osaka on 2 June. On 3 June, the tour started with a visit to Kansai University Senriyama Campus, where the faculty members from the Division of International Affairs hosted a campus tour. Later that afternoon, we met with Prof. Tadakuma and his team at the The University of Osaka Toyonaka campus. They showcased their innovations, inventions, and applications in robotic mechanisms. The following day, we visited Kanadevia A.I/TEC , an IT company dedicated to applying information technology to diverse industries in Japan, including power generation, manufacturing, and construction. The company's representatives shared their expertise and hands-on experience with us, offering insights into real-world projects. After the visit, we also went to the Kyoto University Yoshida Campus. Students and researchers from Kyoto University showed us the projects they were working on and discussed how to implement and apply related technologies, including computer vision and human-robot interaction. On 5 June, the schedule allowed for free cultural exploration, giving students the opportunity to discover Japan independently. This blend of academic learning and cultural immersion can provide a well-rounded perspective on Japan's technological progress and societal values.
