The Japan Study Tour 2025 was organized by the CityU CS department from 2 - 6 June 2025, leading students to visit local universities and companies and engage in cultural activities. The trip was supervised by Dr. Howard Leung and Dr. Kenneth Lee

The journey began with a flight from Hong Kong to Osaka on 2 June. On 3 June, the tour started with a visit to Kansai University Senriyama Campus, where the faculty members from the Division of International Affairs hosted a campus tour. Later that afternoon, we met with Prof. Tadakuma and his team at the The University of Osaka Toyonaka campus. They showcased their innovations, inventions, and applications in robotic mechanisms. The following day, we visited Kanadevia A.I/TEC , an IT company dedicated to applying information technology to diverse industries in Japan, including power generation, manufacturing, and construction. The company's representatives shared their expertise and hands-on experience with us, offering insights into real-world projects. After the visit, we also went to the Kyoto University Yoshida Campus. Students from Kyoto University showed us the projects they were working on and discussed how to implement and apply related technology. On 5 June, the schedule allowed for free cultural exploration, giving students the opportunity to discover Japan independently. This blend of academic learning and cultural immersion can provide a well-rounded perspective on Japan's technological progress and societal values.
