The Schedule

Monday, 2nd June 2025

  • 3:30 PM
    Arrive at Kansai International Airport

    All participants arrived at Kansai International Airport.

  • 4:30 PM
    Transport to Namba

    Group transportation from Kansai International Airport to Namba area.

  • Evening
    Endless Vibes at Torikizoku

    Sky, Mason, Gavan, Oscar, and Alfred had a dinner in Torikizoku.

  • Dining in Shinjuku Yakiniku Gyutan no Lemon

    The group enjoyed a delightful dining experience at Shinjuku Yakiniku Gyutan no Lemon, savoring grilled beef tongue and A5 Japanese black beef. The warm atmosphere, engaging staff, and unique lemonade-making added to the memorable meal.

  • Savouring Osaka's Okonomiyaki

    Tracy enjoyed a delightful evening at Dotonbori Ichiaki, savoring Osaka's famous okonomiyaki. She relished fried noodles, a heart-shaped seafood okonomiyaki with a cute cat design, and Ichiakiyaki with chewy mochi, creating a comforting dining experience after her journey.

Tuesday, 3rd June 2025

  • 8:00 AM
    Departure from Nippombashi Station

    Group gathered at Nippombashi Station for departure to Kansai University.

  • 9:00 AM
    Campus Visit @ Kansai University

    Sharing session and campus tour at Kansai University.

  • 12:00 PM
    Lunch at Kansai University

    Group lunch at Kansai University campus.

  • 1:30 PM
    Campus Visit @ Osaka University

    Visit to Osaka University for academic exchange and campus tour.

  • Evening
    Crab Eating in Osaka

    The group celebrated after a rewarding day by indulging in a full-course crab dinner. They savored crab sashimi, tempura, and grilled crab at the table, culminating in a delicious hot pot and zosui. Each course offered a delightful surprise, showcasing Japan's culinary traditions and creating lasting memories together.

  • Expo '70 Commemorative Park in Osu

    The group visited the Expo '70 Commemorative Park in Osaka, immersing themselves in history and culture. They admired the iconic Tower of the Sun, explored gardens, and reflected on the park's significance, enjoying a meaningful experience filled with memories of innovation and cultural exchange.

  • Amazing Unagi Dinner

    The group enjoyed an unforgettable evening indulging in unagi at a cozy restaurant near Osaka Station. After savoring the rich flavors, they visited the Umeda Sky Building, where breathtaking views of Osaka's skyline at twilight created a magical ending to their day, making it a truly memorable experience.

  • Shubodai Visit

    Sky and Nero and Ken visited Shubodai.

Wednesday, 4th June 2025

  • 8:30 AM
    Departure from Welina Hotel

    Group departed from Welina Hotel for the day's activities.

  • 9:00 AM
    Company Visit @ Kanadevia A.I/TEC

    Visit to Kanadevia for technology exploration and learning.

  • 1:30 PM
    Campus Visit @ Kyoto University

    Visit to Kyoto University for academic exchange and lunch.

  • Evening
    Kamo River Cycling

    The group enjoyed an action-packed day exploring Kyoto, starting with a refreshing bike ride along the Kamo River, taking in scenic views and the vibrant atmosphere. They hiked to Kiyomizu Dera, wandering through historic Sannenzaka and Ninenzaka, before unwinding at a traditional-style Starbucks. After returning to Osaka, they indulged in an all-you-can-eat yakiniku feast in Dotonbori, sharing stories and laughter, making it a memorable conclusion to their adventure.

  • Umeda

    The group enjoyed an exciting evening in Umeda after a relaxing walk along the Kamo River. They explored bustling shopping spots like Hankyu Sanban Gai and found unique Chiikawa merchandise, including keychains and rare dolls. Their adventure culminated in a delicious Wagyu barbecue at Yakiniku Rikimaru, with wagyu nigiri sushi and refreshing highballs. It was a perfect blend of fun shopping and satisfying dining, making Umeda a must-visit destination for unique finds and tasty treats.

Thursday, 5th June 2025

  • All Day
    Suzuka Circuit

    Maya, Ken, Quincy, Nero, Anna, Mason: An exhilarating experience driving on the actual Formula 1 circuit, followed by mini motorbike adventures. The highlight was getting to drive a round trip on the renowned competition field, making it a truly memorable part of the trip.
    Osaka Castle

    Nathan, Charlie, Ivy, Rita: Started with kimono rental near Kuromon Market, then visited Osaka Castle Park. Explored the five-floor, eight-layer main keep with its white walls and golden patterns, took photos at the same background as in the Conan anime, and enjoyed the blend of history and modernity.
    Osaka Expo

    Alfred, Toby, Tracy: Visited various international pavilions including Colombia, Brazil, Peru, and Pasona Natureverse. Explored different cultures and interactive exhibits, with Brazil's pavilion focusing on "The purpose of existing." Ended with a fantastic drone show performing different themes of the Osaka Expo.
    Katsuoji

    Sky, Oscar: Visited the 1,300-year-old "temple of winning luck" and "temple of victory." Made wishes on "Kachi-Daruma" dolls and explored the temple grounds filled with Daruma dolls. Experienced activities including creating Katsuoji postcards and feeding koi carp, while also visiting other temples in the area.

Friday, 6th June 2025

  • All Day
    Nintendo Museum & Fushimi Inari

    Maya, Ken, Quincy, Nero, Anna: Started with a morning visit to Fushimi Inari Taisha, exploring the shrine and buying souvenirs at the Chiikawa shop. Then visited Uji for matcha ice cream and tea shopping. The highlight was the Nintendo Museum in Uji, Kyoto, where they experienced interactive games like Zapper & Scope SP and Ultra Hand SP, saw the Switch 2 on release day, and rediscovered childhood memories with classic Nintendo consoles and controllers.
    Osaka to Tokyo Adventure

    Nathan, Rita, Charlie: Morning shopping at Kuromon Market for souvenirs and local foods. Afternoon archery experience near Tsutenkaku Tower when the tower visit wasn't possible. Evening journey to Tokyo for yakiniku (Japanese BBQ) at a friendly restaurant, experiencing the contrast between Osaka and Tokyo hospitality while enjoying a memorable night in the capital.
