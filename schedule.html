Wednesday, 5th June 2024

  • 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
    MMALab @ University of Tsukuba

    Insights and Innovations: Delving into Advanced Research at the University of Tsukuba's MMALab

  • 4:30 - 6:00 PM
    IT Innovation Inc. Visit

    We visited IT Innovation Inc. A IT company in Japan which provides consulting and project management services and has helped as many as 300 companies reform themselves.

  • 7:00 PM
    Shibuya Crossing & Manga Shop

    After a visit to Innovation Inc., we immersed ourselves in the iconic Shibuya Crossing, famous for its overwhelming flow of pedestrians. This experience, coupled with a visit to a nearby manga store, vividly showcased the bustling urban energy and cultural richness of Tokyo, contrasting sharply with its quieter suburban areas.

    Shinjuku

    We explored the vibrant heart of Tokyo, beginning with the mesmerizing 3D cat billboard in Shinjuku and culminating in the historic charm of Golden Gai. Along the way, we indulged in authentic sushi and experienced the neon-lit nightlife of Kabukicho, capturing the essence of this dynamic city.

Thursday, 6th June 2024

  • Morning
    Exploration of Kamakura & Hase-dera

    Kamakura is the best place for exploring the Japanese culture and photo-taking.

    Sensoji Temple

    On June 6th, our group enjoyed a scenic bike ride to Sensoji Temple in Asakusa, experiencing its vibrant culture and spiritual atmosphere. Later, we savored authentic halal ramen at a nearby restaurant, hosted by a genuine samurai, enriching our cultural and culinary experience in Tokyo.

  • Afternoon
    Oishi Park

    Our group of five students went to the Oishi Park in the Japanese countryside with an organized bus tour on our free day. Although our goal of seeing Mt Fuji was not fulfilled, our journey led us to many more amazing views.

    Kamakura Kokomae Station & Kotoku-in & Enoshima

    From the iconic scenes of Slam Dunk at Kamakura Kokomae to the serene beauty of Kotoku-in's blooming hydrangeas and the vibrant flavors of Enoshima's seafood, our journey was a delightful exploration of culture, nature, and culinary delights.

    A Culinary Journey through Asakusa's Gyumon Halal Ramen

    Asakusa's Gyumon Halal Ramen brings CityU friends together for a taste of Japan's flavors and history. From the welcoming atmosphere to interactive conversations with the flamboyant owner, their culinary journey becomes a cherished memory before venturing into Shinjuku's vibrant streets for more adventures.

  • Evening
    Late-night Snack at Shibuya

    The waiting line for grilled meat is too long, so why not let the matcha desserts and coffee serve as the perfect conclusion to a wonderful day ~
