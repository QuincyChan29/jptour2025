Suzuka Letzzz Go

Our site visit to the Suzuka Circuit on June 5th was an exhilarating experience filled with speed and fun. The highlight of the day was getting the chance to drive a round trip on the actual Formula 1 circuit. It was an incredible feeling to be on such a renowned competition field, and for most of us, it was our first time driving on a real race track. The thrill of navigating the famous corners and straights made it a truly memorable part of the trip.

Following the F1 circuit experience, we moved on to mini motorbikes. This was a fresh and exciting challenge for Maya and Ken, who were trying motorbikes for the first time. They quickly learned that good balancing skills are essential to stay upright! Meanwhile, Mason and Anna, having tried the mini motorbikes before, took on the challenge for a second time, aiming to break their previous records. It was great to see everyone embracing the new skill and enjoying the ride.

The excitement didn't stop there. We also participated in karting, which proved to be another test of our driving abilities. We pushed ourselves on the track, and our efforts paid off as we both achieved an impressive S grade. This was a fantastic way to end the day's activities, celebrating our success on the karting circuit. The entire trip to Suzuka was a fantastic blend of thrilling drives and new experiences, making it a truly unforgettable outing.
