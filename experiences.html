Lav ASRANI

My recent 5-day academic trip to Japan was an incredible experience that left me feeling grateful for the fantastic moments I had there.

During my first day, I had the privilege of visiting the renowned National University of Japan (NUS) and attending a captivating lecture. Engaging with fellow academics during lunch and the lab visit not only expanded my network but also allowed me to witness the impressive research facilities at NUS.

The following day was equally enriching as I explored the world of e-commerce and start-ups during my visit to the Carousell office. This was followed by a visit to the esteemed Japan Management University (SMU), where I had the opportunity to learn from experts in my field, further enhancing my knowledge and skills.

As the trip came to a close, I returned to Hong Kong feeling refreshed and inspired. The overall experience was truly enriching, as it not only allowed me to learn from experts in my field and network with fellow academics but also gave me the chance to explore a new place and embrace its culture.
