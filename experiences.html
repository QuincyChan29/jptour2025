Junaid Ahmed ARIK

The recent study tour in Tokyo, Japan was a profound blend of academic enrichment and cultural immersion. Each day in the technologically advanced and culturally rich country involved ceaseless excitement and a wealth of new academic knowledge.

The day following our arrival, I had the privilege of visiting the esteemed University of Tokyo in the morning, where a comprehensive campus tour gave me a glimpse of the academic life and rigor that the university embodies. In the afternoon, we took an exhilarating shuttle bus ride to the exquisite premises of Waseda University, where I had the opportunity to network with the researchers and learn more about Artificial Intelligence from their research findings.

The next morning at the renowned Tsukuba University was equally stimulating, as I was equipped with exciting insights about Machine Learning, Evolutionary Computation, etc. by enthusiastic research students. The afternoon involved a visit to IT Innovation Inc., which offered a fascinating perspective on the intersection of technology and business goals. I was also amazed by the company’s ambition to achieve a greener Japan in the future, and enlightened by the introduced technologies than can assist the cause.

The fourth day provided a cultural respite. Experiencing the tranquility of Sensoji Temple, engaging with diverse visitors, enjoying a culturally immersive samurai-themed ramen lunch, and exploring the lively Shibuya Crossing gave me an understanding of Tokyo's unique mix of tradition and modernity.

Overall, the blend of academic exchanges and cultural experiences have broadened my horizons, providing invaluable insights that I can carry forward in my academic and professional journey.

- Junaid Ahmed ARIK
